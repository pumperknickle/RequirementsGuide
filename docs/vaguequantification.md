## Avoid Vague Quantification

Avoid the use of vague quantifiers.

> Vague quantifiers tend to make requirements imprecise and ambiguous. They leave room for multiple interpretations, which increases risk.

#### Examples

- some
- any
- allowable
- several
- many
- a lot of
- a few
- almost always
- nearly
- about
- close to
- almost
- approximate
